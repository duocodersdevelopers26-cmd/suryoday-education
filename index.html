Our Story

Dedicated to Your Child's Success

Suryoday Education was founded with a single, powerful belief: every student has the potential to shine brightly — they just need the right guidance, environment, and encouragement.

Located in the heart of Mota Varachha, Surat, we have been the trusted coaching partner for hundreds of families seeking quality education for their children from Standard 1 all the way through to Standard 12 Commerce.

Our experienced faculty members bring not just subject expertise but genuine care for each student's growth — tracking progress individually and adapting teaching methods to ensure every concept is truly understood.
